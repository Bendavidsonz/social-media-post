Caption: "3rd Year Bachelor of Design student Ben Davidson developed this post. He was inspired by the retro aesthetic seen commonly on VHS Casette boxes. We gave the students a few limitations in this assignment. They could only use text, geometric shapes, and colour to explore their thinking about Design and what would be inspiring as a visual for the @designwaikato audience. Ben used the same red to maroon colour palette found on the casette boxes, along with mimicking the font (Arial), adding VHS Grading markings and a grunge effect to really give it that aged feel. Explore the history of art with us! Become a #designstudent on the #BDes"
