Post 3 - Verbs

Aim:

I wanted to play with verbs animating their literal meanings whilst using the word as the text content.

Post Text Content:

Some examples would be:

  • "Motion" and make the text move somehow
  • "Weight" and animate the change of a font's weight
  • "Shape" and animate the change from basic shapes into the letters
